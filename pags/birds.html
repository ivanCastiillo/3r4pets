Nymphs

The nymph is a tender and affectionate exotic bird, for this reason it is one of the preferred psittacines as pets; These can be kept at home without major inconveniences or extremely laborious care, but it is important to carry out certain basic care to guarantee their well-being and quality of life.

Thus, you must monitor some basic aspects such as: food, the hygiene of its cage, how to entertain it and other points of interest so that they can keep you company for a long time.

Parakeets

In their home country of Australia, parakeets live in swarms of up to 2,000 birds. Not surprisingly, this social bird, even in captivity, prefers company. If you are thinking of buying a parakeet, you should consider purchasing at least two copies. These cheerful little birds only feel comfortable with their fellows, with whom they are in constant contact. His repertoire of sounds and body language is extraordinary. Observing them in dealing with other parakeets is fascinating.

Goldfinchs

The goldfinch is a small bird, with beautiful plumage and a very cheerful song, arguments that make it highly appreciated as a pet among fans around the world. In some areas, even singing contests are organized around this beautiful bird.

The care of the goldfinch is not complicated but there are some points to take into account, such as food, the cage and hygiene.
